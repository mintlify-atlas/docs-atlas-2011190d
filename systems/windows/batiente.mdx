Batiente is a planned window system for hinged aluminum windows that open outward at 90°. The sash is attached to the frame with continuous or butt hinges on one vertical edge and secured with a handle/lock mechanism on the opposite stile. This is the most common window typology in residential construction throughout Latin America.

## Planned behaviour

When implemented, the Batiente configurator will accept the rough opening dimensions and calculate:

- **Frame profiles**: outer frame (marco) perimeter cut list
- **Sash profiles**: inner leaf perimeter and intermediate rails if applicable
- **Hinge count** and vertical placement based on sash height
- **Glass light dimensions** with appropriate clearances for the gasket system
- **Handle and lock hardware** quantities

The system will support single-leaf and double-leaf (French window) configurations.
