The **Puerta Corrediza - 2 Hoja** is a two-panel sliding door where both leaves travel along a shared top rail. The panels slide in opposite directions so that the full opening is revealed when both are parked against the adjacent wall sections.

Livewire component: `puertas.corrediza2hoja`

<Warning>
This component is a stub and has not yet been implemented. The current source renders only a placeholder text label. No inputs, calculations, or fabrication summary are available.
</Warning>

## Planned behaviour

Based on the system description in the door selector ("2 Hojas móviles con guiado lineal"), the completed component will work as follows:

- Two equal-width leaves share a single top rail
- Each leaf slides in opposite directions (one left, one right) to open the passage
- The total frame width is split equally between the two leaves
- Both leaves require their own set of frame profiles, glass panels, and roller hardware
