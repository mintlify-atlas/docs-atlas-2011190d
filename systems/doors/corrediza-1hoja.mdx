The **Puerta Corrediza - 1 Hoja** is a single-panel sliding door that travels along a top rail. The panel slides laterally to open, parking over the adjacent wall section.

Livewire component: `puertas.corrediza1hoja`

<Warning>
This component is currently a visual prototype. It renders an animated sliding door demonstration but does not yet include dimension inputs, profile calculations, or a fabrication summary. Full calculation logic is under development.
</Warning>

## Current state

The component renders an interactive schematic that illustrates how the single-leaf sliding door operates:

- A top rail runs the full width of the frame
- The left half of the frame represents the wall (muro) section where the door parks when open
- The right half represents the opening (hueco)
- The door leaf slides between the two positions when clicked

### Interaction

Click anywhere on the door leaf to toggle between **open** and **closed** states. The transition animates over 1200 ms with an ease-in-out curve.

```
Closed → leaf is positioned over the hueco (right half)
Open   → leaf slides left and parks over the muro (left half)
```
