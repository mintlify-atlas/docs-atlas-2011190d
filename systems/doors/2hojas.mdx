The **Puerta 2 Hojas** is a double-leaf hinged aluminum door where both leaves meet at the centre of the frame and are secured by a central closure mechanism.

<Warning>
This door type is not yet available. Selecting "Puerta 2 Hojas" in the application currently displays a "Próximamente" (coming soon) screen. No configuration inputs or calculation logic have been implemented.
</Warning>

## What this door type will be

The Puerta 2 Hojas is described in the system selector as a **doble hoja abatible con cierre central** — a double hinged door with a central latch.

In this configuration:

- The frame opening is divided into two equal-width leaves
- Each leaf is hinged on its outer edge (one on the left, one on the right)
- Both leaves swing inward or outward and meet flush at the centre when closed
- A central closure mechanism (typically a latch or multi-point lock) secures the meeting stile of both leaves
